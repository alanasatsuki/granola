About

Things granola is NOT: only for soccer moms, always expensive, bird food substitute.
Things granola is: nutrient dense, packed with love, makes you want to dance.

My name is Alana Chandler, and I started Granola for Good, a website where you can order my homemade granola, and the profits will go to charity. Win-win!

Three C's Granola

Why the three C's? Chocolate, coconut, cashews. Yum. This granola is made with the classic base—oats, maple syrup, olive/coconut oil—and sassed up with cashews, coconut flakes, and of course, high-quality Dutch-processed cocoa powder.

Ingredients: oats, maple syrup, cocoa powder, egg whites, olive oil, coconut oil, coconut flakes, cashews, almonds, cocoa nibs, vanilla extract, salt

Mediterranean Spice Granola

Millet is an ancient grain (Pssst! It’s actually a seed.) used in Asian and Middle Eastern countries, added to this granola to give it an extra crunch. Tahini (sesame paste), honey, cardamom, and ginger are used to give this batch its unique flavor.

Ingredients: oats, honey, tahini, millet, egg whites, olive oil, almonds, walnuts, coconut flakes, ginger, cardamom, salt
