Three C's Granola

Why the three C's? Chocolate, coconut, cashews. Yum. This granola is made with the classic base—oats, maple syrup, olive/coconut oil—and sassed up with cashews, coconut flakes, and of course, high-quality Dutch-processed cocoa powder.

Ingredients: oats, maple syrup, cocoa powder, egg whites, olive oil, coconut oil, coconut flakes, cashews, almonds, cocoa nibs, vanilla extract, salt

  • Resonant
  • Sweet
  • Crunchy

Matcha Love Granola

As soothing as a steaming cup of tea, this granola will give you your daily dose of matcha, Japanese green tea powder. Sesame seeds are sprinkled in to enhance the toasty flavor of this batch.

Ingredients: oats, honey, matcha, egg whites, olive oil, coconut oil, sesame seeds, almonds, salt

  • Fragrant
  • Nutty
  • Woodsy

Classy Cherry Granola

Classic granola is the type of product that you see in almost every grocery store shelf: oats, fruits, and nuts. But at Granola for Good, instead of having classic granola, we have classy granola. How so? Dried cherries from Traverse City, Michigan. Quinoa, that fancy grain. Cinnamon, the same ingredient used in Queen Elizabeth's tea biscuits.

Ingredients: oats, maple syrup, honey, browned butter, coconut flakes, flax meal, dried cherries, sesame seeds, quinoa, walnuts, almonds, cinnamon, salt

  • Crisp
  • Tart
  • Fruity

Apple Pie Granola

It's apple pie, but 1. granola, 2. healthier, 3. will taste good with yogurt. (Please stick with ice cream for pie, please.) This batch comes loaded with pecans and crispy, dried apples—dehydrated in my own oven!

Ingredients: oats, maple syrup, olive oil, coconut oil, apple chips, pecans, almonds, cinnamon, vanilla

  • Ripe
  • Spiced
  • Golden

    Mediterranean Spice Granola

    Millet is an ancient grain (Pssst! It’s actually a seed.) used in Asian and Middle Eastern countries, added to this granola to give it an extra crunch. Tahini (sesame paste), honey, cardamom, and ginger are used to give this batch its unique flavor.

    Ingredients: oats, honey, tahini, millet, egg whites, olive oil, almonds, walnuts, coconut flakes, ginger, cardamom, salt

    • Smoky
    • Aromatic
    • Creamy

    What is granola nut butter?

    Put simply, it's a nut butter of your choice (almond, cashew, peanut, and maybe even pecan?!) mixed with granola. Big chunks? Nope. Rather, it's blitzed up and mixed in--a match made in heaven.

    Ingredients: Granola for Good granola + nut butter + honey

    • Creamy
    • Rich
    • Spreadable
